Pesto Pasta

A delicious Pesto Pasta Recipe

Following this recipe, you wil learn that it does not take much to create an amazing pasta dish. This dish is very tasty and full of flavor.

Pesto Pasta Ingredients

  • 12 oz. dry spagehetti
  • Salt
  • 3 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/4 cup pine nuts
  • 2 medium garlic cloves (peeled)
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 cup pasta water

Steps

  1. Cook the spagehetti in salted water until ready. Be sure to save 1/2 cup of pasta water before draining.
  2. Use a food processor to create your pesto sauce, add garlic, pine nuts, parmesan and basil leaves. Pour olive oil through the lid, while food processor is running, make sure the ingredients are fully blended. Feel free to use salt to season.
  3. Drain the pasta, reserving 1/2 cup of pasta water. Add pasta to a serving bowl, pour in 1/4 cup pasta water, let it cool for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Mix the pesto with the pasta. Add in more more parmesan and pinenuts by choice.
  5. Ready to serve.
